Laboratory Experiment Tracking System - Dashboard

Web-Based Platform

Develop a web-based platform accessible through standard web browsers, ensuring compatibility across different devices and operating systems.

Utilize responsive design principles to ensure optimal user experience on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile devices.

User Authentication and Authorization

Implement secure user authentication mechanisms to control access to the system based on user roles and permissions.

Define role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict access to sensitive features and data, ensuring confidentiality and data security.

Experiment Management

Create, Update, and Manage Experiments

Provide intuitive interfaces for researchers to create, update, and manage experiments, including fields for experiment details, procedures, protocols, and associated metadata.

Enable tracking of experiment progress, status updates, and experimental results, allowing researchers to monitor and analyze their experiments in real-time.

Inventory and Resource Management

Track Laboratory Equipment, Chemicals, Samples, and Consumables

Incorporate inventory management features to track laboratory equipment, chemicals, samples, and consumables.

Implement barcode or RFID tracking capabilities for efficient inventory management and automated data capture.

Data Visualization and Analysis

Integrate data visualization tools to analyze experimental data, generate graphs, charts, and reports, and identify trends or correlations.

Provide interactive dashboards and customizable reporting functionalities to enable researchers to gain insights from their data and make informed decisions.

Search and Filtering

Implement robust search functionality to quickly locate experiments, inventory items, or specific data based on user-defined criteria.

Enable advanced filtering options to refine search results and navigate large datasets efficiently.

Notifications and Alerts

Incorporate automated notifications and alerts to inform users about important events, such as experiment milestones, inventory updates, or overdue tasks.

Allow users to customize notification preferences and subscribe to relevant alerts to stay informed.
