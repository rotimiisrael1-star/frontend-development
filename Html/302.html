Laboratory Experiment Tracking System

  • Form or interface for creating new experiments, including fields for experiment title, description, date, researcher(s), associated laboratory equipment, and experimental procedures.
  • Ability to view and edit existing experiments, including updating experimental data and status.
  • Inventory management features to track laboratory equipment, chemicals, samples, and other resources.
